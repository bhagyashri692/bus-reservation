To make a bus reservation, you'll need to provide the following information: 
Passenger details: The name, age, and other details of the passenger
Travel dates and times: The date and time of travel, including the starting and destination locations
Bus details: The bus number and seat number
Payment information: The payment method and any applicable fees
You can make bus reservations online using a website or app. Some systems also allow you to manage your bookings and check in online.
